About This Project

How did Plant.co's 2023 product quantity performance and quality trends compare to the previous year, and which regions, product categories, or accounts require strategic adjustments to improve profitability?

Plant.co 2023 Performance Dashboard Analysis

The dashboard highlights Outdoor and Landscape products as growth drivers (YTD vs PYTD), requiring prioritized inventory scaling, while France and China show underperformance (e.g., France units: 20K, 5K, 4K, 2K), necessitating region-specific marketing or supply chain adjustments.

Quality metrics peak at 79%, signaling risks to customer satisfaction or margins, with Increase or Decrease flags guiding corrective actions. Profitability segmentation identifies recurring high-value accounts (Value YTD) and opportunities to balance low-quantity or high-QP% accounts.

Strategic next steps include:

  • Optimizing stock for high-growth categories.
  • Auditing quality processes to reduce costs.
  • Launching targeted campaigns in lagging regions to revive sales.

This analysis bridges data-driven insights (YTD/PYTD trends, quality metrics) with actionable strategies to boost profitability and market share.

About This Project

Question: Which coffee products drive the most revenue, and how do customer loyalty programs and geographic regions influence these trends?

Data Driven Solution

Brew Insights is a dynamic Excel dashboard designed to optimize coffee sales by analysing revenue drivers, customer loyalty, and geographic trends. Business teams gain actionable intelligence such as:

  • Prioritizing Arabica inventory.
  • Targeting U.S. markets for premium roasts.
  • Enhancing loyalty programs to improve retention.

Technical users benefit from PivotTables, slicers, and conditional formatting for deep dives into sales patterns. The solution bridges analytics and operations, enabling data-driven decisions (Example: seasonal promotions, regional stock adjustments and ROI optimization).

By aligning technical rigor with strategic goals, Brew Insights empowers cross-functional teams to boost revenue, reduce waste, and improve customer retention.

British Airways Analysis

View Tableau Report

About This Project

Question: How can British Airways enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty by identifying underperforming service areas and traveller segments to prioritize improvements and tailor offerings?

Solution

This dashboard addresses declining customer satisfaction by analysing 2016–2023 review data across key metrics like food, seat comfort, value for money and segments like business travellers, aircraft types, & regions. For instance, low value-for-money scores in Economy Class highlight pricing or amenity gaps.

Recommendations include:

  • Upgrading seat comfort in long-haul aircraft (e.g., Boeing 777).
  • Refining meal options for premium classes.
  • Launching region-specific promotions based on feedback.

By leveraging customer feedback analysis, British Airways can focus on high-impact upgrades like seat comfort and meal quality to boost satisfaction, loyalty, and repeat bookings.
